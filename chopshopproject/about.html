Where did we begin?

The Chop Shop was founded by Jimmy-John Bowsmith William Washington. Mr. Washington and his family began building The Chop Shop from an old petrol station in the winter of 1951. Over the course of the next year, they toiled to make deadlines. Eventually, their new restaurant opened on a snowy night in December of 1952. Since then, the business has been running strong, delivering only the finest ingredients and service. We have always and will always keep to our family values of good service, good food, and a passion for motorcylces! See you soon!

-Bobby Washington
