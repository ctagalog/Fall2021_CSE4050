Ride for charity event!

If you are a rider please join us as we give back to our community with a "Ride for charity" Events. The money raised by participation in these events goes to helping homeless children in the san bernardino area. Along with that visiting the chopshop during these events also benefits these charities. This is because during said event the chopshop donates 30% of all proceeds on to charity as well. Join us today! Help us bring an end to child homelessness.

  • December 6th - the date of the charity ride
  • 30% of proceeds will be donated from the period of Dec 1st - Dec 12th
