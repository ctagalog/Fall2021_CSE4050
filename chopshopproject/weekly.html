Veteran Apreciation Tuesday

We at Chop Shop always have admiration and respect for the heroes of our country and we show it on this day.

  • During tuesday veterans will recieve 50% off of all thier order.
  • All acompanying friend and family members also get a 25% off discount.
  • We also offer free beverages to all veterans on this day.

Seniors Apreciation Thursday

Chop Shop always pay's our respects to seniors for they have thought us many things and this day is for them.

  • Senior citizens get a 25% discount on all thier orders on this day.
  • They also get a free drink of thier choice.
  • We have special recommended menu only available on this day that changes every time.
